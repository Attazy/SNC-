Company Overview

Short profile of who we are and what we deliver.

Founded in 2009, Sentra Netcomindo started as a Network Infrastructure Provider and has delivered comprehensive solutions from networking products to cabling solutions ever since.

Today, Sentra Netcomindo expands its services to offer a wider range of IT solutions in response to growing customer needs, especially in Financial Institutions, Telecommunications, and Public Services.
